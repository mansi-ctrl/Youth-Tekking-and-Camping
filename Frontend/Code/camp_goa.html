Backpacking To Goa


Goa is definitely the best tourist place for India. "The pearl of the east," Goa is known for its Gothic churches, crumbling forts, palm-fringed beaches, coconut groves, ferry rides, bubbly folk music… the list is endless! With its 131-km-long coastline, Goa is an important locale in every tourist's itinerary.

Trip to Goa, driving through beaches, forests to explore exotic locations by our SUV for 7 days , yes its backpacking road trip. Sounds like your Cup of Tea? We are foodies and yes we are adventure freaks. We are not gonna book a lodge or star hotels but yes we carry our tents and sleeping bags and yes we gonna roll in our SUV.

Bucket list : boating, paragliding, scuba-diving, cruise, camping everywhere are in our list too.

Participants will have to follow the instructions clearly given by the volunteer/guide, Misbehave/Arguing with the volunteers/guide/instructors will be considered as disciplinary issues
Smoking, Alcohol, Tobacco & Abusive Language are strictly prohibited and if anyone found a suspect in such cases, the participation will be terminated and no further service/return traveling/refund will be offered then after
Accommodation & Sanitary Facilities for Girls & Boys are separate
Punctuality is taken as a serious matter

Schedule Changes

In case of unforeseen conditions, the schedule/itinerary can be modified/canceled
Invincible reserves all rights to cancel/shorten/modify the itinerary, and the final decision is authorized with the trustee team

Covid Measures


  1. Using Face Mask/Face Cover is compulsory
  2. Aarogya Setu App on your mobile phone is mandatory, before commencing Bus journey & during the camping duration.
  3. Maintain Social Distancing and Wash Hands Frequently, Avoid handshakes with other participants and staff members.
  4. Must Carry personal hand sanitisers to use during activities/camping
  5. The Participants must carry personal items for safe camping like Plate/lunch box for meals, mug, spoons, water bottle.
  6. Try to avoid touching at any place like handlebars, supporting bars in a bus, automated water taps, sanitiser machines, etc.
  7. Use dustbins for garbage and mask disposal.

Campsite & Washroom Area

  1. The whole campsite to be cleaned and sanitised daily.
  2. Close fall ins, campfire, Musical Evenings to be avoided.
  3. Every participant would pass through a temperature test to ensure the safety and spread of infection.

Tents

  1. All the tents would be sanitised properly before the commencement of the camp and once checked out, they will not be allocated for at least 5 days to anyone.
  2. 1 person per tent would be allocated if he is a solo traveller. If family members requests for a separate tent then will be allocated

Kitchen and Dining

  1. Masks and Hand gloves are compulsory for the cooking staff
  2. Participants will be advised carrying personal plates/lunchboxes, spoon & mug to stay contact-free.
  3. Participants are encouraged to enjoy meals in open areas and if the dining area is used, then keep a minimum of 6 feet distance between 2 people.
  4. Kitchen and Dining area to be cleaned and sanitised frequently, at least twice a day.
